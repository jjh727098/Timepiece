Half A Decade In The Making

From CAD

simulations to stress tests, we meticulously
crafted ACF-01, ensuring precision, durability
and optimal performance.

  • 2018
    The Beginning

    It started with the obsession of a modern timepiece driven by design and technique, to create a different timekeeping experience. Purest form, without excessive ornaments.

  • 2019
    Inspired By Icons of Automotive, Racing and Aeronautics Engineering.
  • 2020
    From Concept To Timepiece, A Long Challenging Process

    The timepiece's design philosophy can be summarized in three fundamental principles: lightness, comfort, and an unrelenting pursuit of technique.

  • 2021
    Pioneering Double-Wishbone Suspension with Precision and Flexibility.
  • 2022
    Assessment of the Timepiece’s Different Aspects: Design, Calibre, Ergonomy

    Development of the case and other specific components such as crown, screws by FSmicromec. Glass by Kyburz to deliver both anti-reflective optics, scratch resistance and contact damage.

  • 2024
    ACF-01 Debut
  • 2023
    Continued Development
